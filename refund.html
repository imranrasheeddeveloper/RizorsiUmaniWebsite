Refund Policy

We want our clients to be satisfied with the temporary employees we provide through our platform. If you need to cancel a booking, we have the following policy in place:

  • If you cancel your booking more than 24 hours before the start time, you will receive a full refund. You will be responsible for the refund processing cost of the payment processor. The refund will be processed within 24 hours of your request and may take up to 2 weeks to be completed.
  • If you cancel your booking within 24 hours of the start time, no refund will be given.

We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time. If you have any questions or concerns about our refund policy, please don't hesitate to contact us.
